Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

At Kegex, your privacy is our top priority. We believe that your health data belongs only to you. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Kegex is designed to function with minimal data collection. We do not require you to create an account to use the core features of the app.

  • Local Progress: Your training history, levels, and statistics are stored locally on your device.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which buttons are clicked) to improve the app. This data cannot be traced back to you.

2. Subscriptions

Subscription payments are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not see or store your credit card information or personal billing details.

3. Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your health or personal data to outside parties.

4. Your Consent

By using Kegex, you consent to our privacy policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy, contact us at support@kegex.app.
